Goals for CrowdSound

CrowdSound.us is a web application created and owned by Jared Frank
As of now the project is still in its early stages due to time contraints to work on it as well as the undertaking that would be needed in order to get the site to work at the capacity that it is intended to.
The goal for expanding the site is to begin on college campuses all across the United States so that students or organizations that are hosting parties can take advantage of the application on a smaller scale are first.
Once the issues and scalability of the site have been figured out, the hope is that the application will either grow to be big enough to be used at large events such as: sporting events, fundraisers, concerts, etc. But as of now those are just ideas for the future.
